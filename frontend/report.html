Submit a detailed report with location, issue category, and a short description. The platform supports potholes, accidents, fallen trees, blocked roads, and roadside garbage.

Suggested incident types

  • Road surface damage such as potholes or cracks
  • Traffic incidents, vehicle collisions, or road obstruction
  • Fallen trees or branches blocking traffic
  • Garbage piles, illegal dumping, or waste spillovers
  • Urgent hazards that need immediate public maintenance
