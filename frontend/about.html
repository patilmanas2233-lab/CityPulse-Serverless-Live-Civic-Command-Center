How this civic reporting experience works

CityPulse combines a polished public-facing portal with a simple reporting workflow. Residents submit issues, city teams review them, and the dashboard updates statuses live so everyone can see what is pending, in progress, or resolved.

Fast submission 1 min Capture location, category, and photo in a single flow.
Clear visibility Live feed Each issue appears in the home feed with its current handling status.
Better action Priority Crews can focus on urgent hazards first and reduce response delays.
